About AIBIoT by Maverick Software

Revolutionizing Business Intelligence with AI-Powered Insights

AIBIoT is a cloud-based platform that integrates data from multiple sources—IoT devices, SQL databases, CSV files, and external APIs—to deliver advanced, AI-powered business insights.

Designed for both small-to-medium businesses and enterprises, AIBIoT combines real-time analytics with historical data analysis, enabling organizations to make data-driven decisions with accuracy.

AI-Powered Dashboard

AIBIoT features a user-friendly, customizable dashboard that displays live IoT sensor readings and critical business metrics such as revenue, user engagement, and website traffic.

Predictive Analytics & Anomaly Detection

Using advanced machine learning algorithms, including Linear Regression and ARIMA forecasting models, AIBIoT predicts future trends with confidence intervals, helping businesses anticipate changes.

The platform also employs AI-powered anomaly detection, ensuring unexpected issues are flagged instantly through real-time alerts via SMS and email notifications.

AI-Driven Business Recommendations

AIBIoT generates actionable recommendations tailored to business needs, helping with marketing strategies, inventory management, and operational scaling.

Scalability & Customization

With high-level customization and white-label capabilities, AIBIoT adapts to evolving business needs, providing a scalable solution for unlocking data-driven growth.
